About Me

I’m Seun Abiodun, a passionate Frontend Developer, WordPress Specialist, and Digital Marketer based in Nigeria. Over the last three years, I’ve been helping businesses, non-profits, and startups transform their ideas into modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites.

My tech journey started with a curiosity for how websites are built. From learning HTML and CSS, I advanced into JavaScript, WordPress, and digital marketing. This combination allows me not only to build visually appealing websites, but also to ensure they are optimised for speed, SEO, and conversions.

I’ve worked on diverse projects such as educational platforms, logistics websites, corporate portals, and non-profit organisations. Each project sharpened my ability to understand client needs and deliver tailored solutions that align with their goals.

Beyond development, I’m also passionate about teaching and empowering others with digital skills. I believe technology should be an enabler, and I aim to contribute to the growth of a thriving digital ecosystem in Africa.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me exploring Web3 trends, improving my design skills, or learning about digital marketing strategies that drive growth.
